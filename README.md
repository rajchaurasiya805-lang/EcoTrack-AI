# EcoTrack AI 🌱

EcoTrack AI is an AI-powered Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform that helps users track, understand, and reduce their carbon emissions through personalized insights, sustainability challenges, and interactive analytics.

- Carbon Footprint Calculator
- AI Eco Advisor

- Responsive Design

## Problem Statement

Climate change awareness is increasing, but most people do not understand how their daily activities contribute to carbon emissions. EcoTrack AI provides an easy-to-use platform that calculates emissions, visualizes impact, and offers practical recommendations.

## How It Works

1. Enter transportation, electricity, and lifestyle data.
2. Calculate carbon footprint score.
3. View benchmark comparisons.
4. Complete sustainability challenges.
5. Earn badges and track progress.
6. Receive AI-powered recommendations.
